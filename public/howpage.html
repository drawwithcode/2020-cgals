When you start, you'll see the contribution of the last user who collaborated to Memorypedia.
It could be a word, a picture or a drawing.

You'll have 1 minute to think of a way for continuing the chain by using one of these media.
By selecting the way you want to collaborate, you'll land in the page you've chosen:

(1) For uploading a picture.
(2) For inserting a word.
(3) To draw your sketch in real time.

By clicking "add to the chain", your contribution will be saved in the database.
Then you'll be redirected to the final page, where you'll be able to see the full chain, from the first
piece to the last.
